How it works
No manual setup after a meeting is created.
Step 1

Calendar sync. A new event appears on the connected calendar and the system picks it up automatically.

Step 2

Sheet + QR generated. Everyone from the master participant list is added, marked Absent, and a secure QR code is issued.

Step 3

Live check-in. Participants scan in, the dashboard updates instantly, and reports are ready the moment the meeting ends.
